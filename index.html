Swing Trading Strategy Checker

About this strategy
This tool identifies institutional-style buy setups by combining momentum reset and trend resumption.

Key concepts:
• Institutional Buy Zone → price resets but holds above fair value.
• Momentum Reset (RoC negative) → short-term weakness that shakes out retail traders.
• Trend Resumption (Close > AVWAP) → price reclaiming anchored VWAP, often used by institutions.
• Risk Management → entry, stop loss, target, and position sizing ensure discipline.

Conditions:
1. RoC(10d) < 0 → momentum reset.
2. Latest Close > AVWAP → trend resumption.
3. Both together → PASS ✅ (potential institutional buy setup).
4. If either fails → FAIL ❌ (no setup).
