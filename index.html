Swing Trading Strategy Checker

About This Strategy:
This tool identifies institutional-style buy setups
Key Concepts:
• Institutional Buy Zone → price resets but holds above fair value.
• Momentum Reset (RoC negative) → short-term weakness that shakes out retail traders.
• Trend Resumption (Close > AVWAP) → price reclaiming anchored VWAP, often used by institutions.
• Risk Management → entry, stop loss, target, and position sizing ensure discipline.

Conditions:
1. RoC(10d) < 0 → momentum reset.
2. Latest Close > AVWAP → trend resumption.
3. Both together → PASS ✅ (potential institutional buy setup).
4. If either fails → FAIL ❌ (no setup).
Glossary:
• RoC (Rate of Change) → Measures momentum by comparing today’s price to price N days ago.
• AVWAP (Anchored VWAP) → Average price since a key swing high, used by institutions to gauge fair value.
• Stop Loss → Price level where you exit to limit losses.
• Target → Price level where you take profit, usually set at 2× risk.
• Position Size → Number of units you buy, based on risk % and capital.
Sample Walkthrough:
If you had ₹25,000 and risk 2%:
• Risk capital = 25,000 × 0.02 = ₹500.
• Suppose entry = ₹100 and stop loss = ₹95 → risk per unit = ₹5.
• Position size = 500 ÷ 5 = 100 units.
• Target = Entry + 2×Risk = 100 + 10 = ₹110.
This shows how capital, risk %, stop loss, and target combine into a disciplined trade plan.
